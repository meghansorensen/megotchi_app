# 🐾 Megotchi

A cute modern Tamagotchi-inspired mobile app where you raise, customize, and build a friendship with your very own digital creature.

Megotchi is a fun side project built to explore mobile development, game mechanics, animations, social features, and creating a tiny world that grows with you.

## ✨ Vision

Megotchi brings the classic virtual pet experience into a modern mobile app.

Users can:

* Raise their own Megotchi creature
* Build its personality through interactions
* Customize its world
* Make friends with other Megotchis
* Play games together
* Watch their creature evolve over time

The goal is simple:

> Create a little digital companion that makes you smile when you open the app.
